About Odor Sources in Melissa

Melissa, TX has three documented odor-producing facilities plus a cattle feedlot. Residents have filed complaints with TCEQ and the City since at least 2016. The City Manager has publicly acknowledged the city has no jurisdiction over these facilities.

Wind patterns shift seasonally — south/SSW winds in spring & summer push landfill odors north into residential areas, while north/NW winter fronts push the Organic Recycler's odors south into Auburndale and nearby subdivisions.

Odor radii are estimates based on EPA guidance. This is a decision-support tool, not a scientific assessment.
